Pork Sisig

Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish. It can be considered as a main dish or an appetizer.Sisig originated from Pampanga. This place is considered as the culinary capital of the Philippines. It was invented by Lucia Cunanan. She is popularly known as Aling Lucing and the Sisig queen.

Pancit Malabon

Pancit Palabok is a variation of pancit, a classic dish many Filipinos enjoy. A favorite at workplace parties, birthdays, and other celebrations, Pancit Palabok is one of the staple noodles in Filipino cuisine.Tasty and filling, this pancit contains a savory sauce that will make your mouth water.
